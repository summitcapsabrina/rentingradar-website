Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

What is rental arbitrage?

Rental arbitrage is a real estate strategy where you lease a property on a long-term basis and then sublease it as a short-term rental on platforms like Airbnb and VRBO. You earn the difference between your lease cost and your short-term rental revenue — without ever owning the property.

Can I get expert help while I learn?

Yes — meet Sabrina, your built-in Rental Arbitrage chat expert. She's trained specifically on rental arbitrage, deal calculation, and operations management, so she can answer in-depth questions about both the strategy and the platform and guide you to becoming a RentingRadar pro in no time. If a question goes beyond her, she'll hand you off to a human who knows even more.

Do I need to install anything?

No. RentingRadar runs entirely in your browser at app.rentingradar.com. Just sign in with Google and you're ready to go — no downloads or installations required. There's also an optional Google Chrome extension that lets you import listings and auto-fill property details straight from popular rental sites like Zillow, Apartments.com, and HotPads.

How does the Deal Calculator work?

The Deal Calculator uses competitor data from comparable Airbnb listings to project revenue across best, likely, and worst-case scenarios. It then calculates your Net Operating Income, cash flow, cash-on-cash return, and payback period. Each property also gets a Deal Temperature score — Slam-Dunk, Potential, Risky, or No-Go — so you can instantly evaluate profitability.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Your data is stored securely on Google Cloud infrastructure and tied to your Google account. Each user's data is completely isolated and private. We never sell, share, or access your property data.

Can I import listings from rental sites?

Yes — and it's one of the fastest ways to build your pipeline. Our Google Chrome extension auto-fills property details straight from Zillow, Apartments.com, and HotPads, so you can add a property in seconds with no manual data entry. Prefer to do it yourself? You can also add properties with our streamlined entry form or bulk-import via CSV or Excel. Standard and Pro plans include data import/export.

Can I cancel or downgrade anytime?

Absolutely. There are no contracts or commitments. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time. Your data stays safe regardless of your plan.
